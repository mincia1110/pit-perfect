# Game Rules

The player controls two wheel-gun operators:

- Operator A services the front wheel.
- Operator B services the rear wheel.

Both operators work on the same side first. Only after both front and rear wheel workflows are complete may the player trigger crossover. They then service the opposite side. Four-wheel simultaneous service is intentionally not implemented.

## Phase Order

1. `READY`
2. `ARRIVAL`
3. `BRAKING`
4. `JACK_UP`
5. `FIRST_SIDE_SERVICE`
6. `CROSSOVER`
7. `SECOND_SIDE_SERVICE`
8. `JACK_DOWN`
9. `RELEASE`
10. `FINISHED`

Mistakes add time penalties rather than ending the run.
